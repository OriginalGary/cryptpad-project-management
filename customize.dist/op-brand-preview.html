[ Why Open Paws ]

Built for activists, not corporations

01 End-to-end encrypted

The server cannot read your data. Keys live in your URL fragment — not transmitted to the server, not stored server-side.

02 Investigation-safe

Designed for ag-gag legal environments. Real deletion, no soft deletes. Supports device seizure preparation workflows.

03 Coalition-ready

Granular sharing controls for multi-org coordination. Your data stays yours even when working across coalitions.

04 Self-hosted

Run on your own infrastructure. Zero dependency on commercial cloud providers who may comply with industry warrants.
